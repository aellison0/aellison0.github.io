A Visual Look at Our Favorite Movies

by Aja Ellison

There are movies we think are good and movies we all pay to go see. These movies are not always the same. This is a visual look at our favorite movies, using IMDB ratings and the data of the box office ticket sales (adjusted for inflation) from Box Office Mojo.

International Ranking for Box Office Ticket Sales

The International Ranking for Box Office Ticket Sales were relatively close based on population. China, which had the largest population (1.3 billion), brought in 6.6 million in box office sales. Hong Kong, which has a smaller population, brought in 300 million for box office ticket sales.

Number of Movies in the IMDB Top 250 by Decade

From 1920-2010, there have been a plethora of movies that have made the top 250 rankings.

The 1920's had a total of 6 movies that made the IMDB top 250.

The 1930's had a total of 7 movies that made the IMDB top 250.
