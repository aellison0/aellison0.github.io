A Visual Look at Our Favorite Movies

by Aja Ellison

There are movies we think are good and movies we all pay to go see. These movies are not always the same. This is a visual look at our favorite movies, using the IMDB ratings and data of the box office ticket sales (adjusted for inflation) from Box Office Mojo.
